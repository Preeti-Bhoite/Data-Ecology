# Data Ecology

This repository contains academic coursework completed as part of the Data Ecology module.

The module explores the relationship between data, technology, governance, ethics, and society, with a particular focus on healthcare and artificial intelligence.

## Key Topics
- Data governance and regulation
- Ethics of data and AI
- Responsible AI
- Healthcare data ecosystems
- Public policy and data-driven decision making

## Featured Work
**Evaluative Review**

*Governing the Brain: How Should the UK Government Promote and Control AI Developments for Patient Stratification in Alzheimer's Disease Clinical Trials?*

- Critically evaluated the role of AI in patient stratification for clinical trials
- Analysed UK government policy, regulation, and ethical considerations
- Discussed risks, benefits, and governance challenges in healthcare AI
- Connected data science practices with public trust and patient safety

## Purpose
This repository highlights my ability to critically analyse data-driven technologies within ethical, regulatory, and societal contexts.
